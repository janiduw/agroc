Who Are we

A Team of Professionals are engaged in facilitating the needs of exporters, importers and producers of agricultural plants and related material in compliance with the condition in the Plant Protection Act of Sri Lanka.

Our Team of Agro Consultant scientists consisting of expert Agronomists, entomologist, pathologist, extentionists and other specialists with long years of practical experience in the field of agriculture within the state sector will analyze and provide timely solutions in respect of diverse agricultural and plant quarantine problems encountered by various sectors .

Consultancy and services

Feasiblity Studies

Advise on feasibility study on technical and other aspects related to the proposed projects concerning the to import/export of seeds and planting material, plant products etc.

Site inspections

Site inspection of intended plant material and produce consignments at nurseries, warehouses and factories etc. to ensure the necessary standards quality assurance and specifications in terms of applicable quarantine laws

Advise

Advise on phyto-sanitory measures, and other prescribed procedures for pre-export treatments to be adopted to ensure quality standards of the consignment acceptable to countries of relevance.

Training

Training of technical staff to undertake assignments on the export of plant material, routine maintenance of premises with a view to avoiding adverse contaminations.
